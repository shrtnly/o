# Password Reset Workflow

Follow these steps to reset your password if you've forgotten it:

1. **Initiate Reset**:
   - Go to the [Login Page](/auth).
   - Click on the **"পাসওয়ার্ড ভুলে গেছেন?"** link below the password field.

2. **Provide Email**:
   - Enter your registered email address in the input field.
   - Click the **"রিসেট লিঙ্ক পাঠান"** button.

3. **Check Your Email**:
   - Open your email inbox (and check the Spam folder if needed).
   - Look for an email from **BeeLesson** with a password reset link.
   - Click the link inside the email.

4. **Set New Password**:
   - You will be redirected to the **"নতুন পাসওয়ার্ড সেট করুন"** page.
   - Enter your new password (minimum 6 characters).
   - Click the **"পাসওয়ার্ড পরিবর্তন করুন"** button.

5. **Login**:
   - After a success message, you will be redirected back to the [Auth Page](/auth).
   - Log in using your email and the **new password**.

---
*Note: The reset link is valid for a limited time. If it expires, please initiate the process again.*
